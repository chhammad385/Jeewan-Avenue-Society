👥 Role Descriptions & Responsibilities

🏆 President

Access Level: Full Administrative Access

  • User management and role assignment
  • Staff member management
  • Financial oversight and approval
  • Announcements and communications
  • Feedback review and response
  • Renter documentation management

🤝 Vice-President

Access Level: No President Console Access

  • Supports President in decision making
  • Access to standard member features only
  • Can view society information

📋 General-Secretary

Access Level: High Administrative Access

  • User profile management
  • Staff coordination and management
  • Financial record management
  • Communication coordination
  • Feedback management
  • Document and lease management

💼 Finance-Secretary

Access Level: Financial Focus

  • Financial record management
  • Budget tracking and reporting
  • Payment processing oversight
  • Financial approvals

📢 Information-Secretary

Access Level: Communication Focus

  • Staff member coordination
  • Announcement management
  • Community communications
  • Feedback collection and review
  • Information dissemination

🏠 Society Member

Access Level: Member Features Only

  • View personal profile
  • View society information
  • Submit feedback
  • View announcements
  • Access member services
